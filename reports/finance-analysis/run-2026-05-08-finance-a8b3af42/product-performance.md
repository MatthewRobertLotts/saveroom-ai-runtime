## Operational interpretation
- Japanese performs very well when attached to recognisable, repeatable lines.
- Korean is not prominent in the top contributors inside this file, which suggests either lower sales share or weaker merchandising in this period.
- Accessories and filler items can help engagement, but they should not be relied on as standalone revenue drivers.
- “Card on screen” style entries create activity, but they are weak commercial performers unless used to trigger larger follow-on basket behaviour.
