# Fee Breakdown

## Totals
- GMV: **£11,726.09**
- Platform commission: **£756.44**
- Payment processing: **£830.18**
- Total fees: **£1,586.62**
- Net after fees: **£10,139.47**

## Fee rates
- Platform commission rate: **6.45%**
- Payment processing rate: **7.08%**
- Combined fee rate: **13.53%**

## Observations
- Payment processing is slightly larger than platform commission in this sample, which means low-ticket orders are getting hit from both sides.
- At a **£5.76** AOV, fee pressure is still too high for comfortable scaling.
- Several products with meaningful volume are running at **16% to 32%** fee drag, purely because selling price is too low.

## Highest fee-pressure lines with repeat volume
- **Future Flash Booster Pack**: 20 orders, AOV **£1.20**, fee rate **31.63%**
- **Soulburning Volcano 1st Edition**: 11 orders, AOV **£1.18**, fee rate **31.85%**
- **40 x Card Sleeves**: 11 orders, AOV **£1.18**, fee rate **29.92%**
- **Stellar Miracle Booster Pack**: 11 orders, AOV **£1.45**, fee rate **27.12%**
- **Mega Symphonia Booster Pack**: 18 orders, AOV **£1.72**, fee rate **23.87%**

## Interpretation
The fee problem is structural, not incidental. SaveRoom can continue using community-friendly pricing, but it needs stronger bundle conversion and better product grouping to stop low-value orders from leaking margin.
